MY GALLERY

Beautiful gray clouds dominate the sky.
Two dolphins swim in the crystal clear blue water.
The sun dips low on the horizon, casting the sky in a vibrant blend of fiery orange, soft pink and deep purple hues.
The evening moon hangs in the clouds, its surface marked with faint craters and soft shadows.
In a moonless sky, the milky way stretches like a luminous ribbon.
As the sun rises up in the sky, the world awakens as the horizon begins to glow softly and the air feels fresh.
